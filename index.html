Randomly Generated Mixing Fees

Every mixing operation has a fee of 1% - 3%

At MixGuru protection our users’ privacy and anonymity is our primary focus. As a result, in order to make it as challenging as possible to track transactions via Blockchain analysis, we have devised a system of randomly generated fees for bitcoin mixing activities.

To make more difficult for anyone to calculate the total amount combined, we charge a fee mixing operation that ranges from 1% to 3% and is generated with a decimal value with four digits.

The user also pays the miner’s fee required by the crypto network, which is substracted from the combined total.

This guarantees that any chance of counter calculations on the blockchain is completely eliminated and that your anonymity is maintained with the highest level of security.
